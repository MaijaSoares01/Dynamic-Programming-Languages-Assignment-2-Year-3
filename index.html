How does it work?

Send me a photo or description of what you want to see in drawing (color or black and white):

  • Portraits
  • Original characte
  • Fanart
  • Video game characters

If you are not sure if I can make the drawing, contact me by message and I will inform you as soon as possible about the possibilities!
Then choose from the options what you want (portrait(head to shoulder), half body(head to middle torso) or full body). Adding extra people or pets includes an extra cost.

EDITING AND REFUNDING

Editing and refunding are all acceptable. However:

  • You will be charged an initial amount of 50% before the digital process starts
  • Once the piece is finished, I will be open to changes
  • Once the piece is to the client's liking they will then be charged the rest of the 50% or extra if more was asked
  • If you wish for a refund but the piece is drawn already, a refund will be declined and the client wont be charged the extra 50% and they will not recieve the piece
  • If you wish for a refund and the piece is not drawn, a refund will be accepted

Digital art

Visual art form

Digital art is an artistic work or practice that uses digital technology as part of the creative or presentation process. Since the 1960s, various names have been used to describe the process, including computer art and multimedia art. Digital art is itself placed under the larger umbrella term new media art
